Tamper evident labels for bottles and secure QR codes for anti-counterfeiting

iHealth uses tamper evident labels and secure QR codes to ensure authenticity of their probiotic supplement products while monitoring for scans outside the intended market.

Key features and benefits

  • Tamper-evident label with secure QR codes for anti-counterfeiting
  • Dashboards and alerts for counterfeit product detection
  • Unique, serialized QR codes redirect to custom mobile landing pages displaying supply chain data
  • Multi-region support (English, Mandarin Chinese) and targeted promotions e.g. WeChat official account, etc.
  • End-user demographic data (gender, geo, product preferences, etc.)

Solutions used

  • Product digital identity
  • Anti-counterfeiting
  • Customer engagement
  • Supply chain traceability

Project highlights and results

iHealth used anti-counterfeit packaging including tamper evident labels and secure QR codes to detect gray market and counterfeit products in China. The solution also provides unit-to-case aggregation with the Scantrust Enterprise app for supply chain traceability.
